**The scheme you've never heard of, twice the size of the one you have — £67 bn in RO subsidy paid by UK consumers since 2002.**

Of that headline figure: a substantial fraction is attributable to **biomass** (Drax co-firing 2003-2016 plus Drax + Lynemouth dedicated biomass conversions); £44.0M was distributed to ROC-holders during the **2021-22 mutualisation event** when suppliers defaulted on ROC obligations during the energy-price crisis; **NIRO** Northern Ireland obligations are excluded from the GB headline but available via `country='NI'` filter in the published Parquet. ## Shared Patterns

### Chart embed convention (PNG-first + interactive HTML link)

**Source:** `docs/schemes/ro.md` lines 42–44 (representative; repeats at 57–59, 77–79, 94–96).
**Apply to:** All 4 chart embeds on `docs/schemes/cfd.md`.

```markdown
![<alt text>](../charts/html/<chart_name>_twitter.png)

[Interactive version](../charts/html/<chart_name>.html){target="_blank"}
```

Non-negotiable: PNG appears first on its own line (Twitter-card rendering); blank line; then `[Interactive version](...)` link with `{target="_blank"}` attribute. Matches D-05 exactly. Do NOT use pymdownx tabs or iframes.

### Material `grid cards` convention

**Source:** `docs/themes/cost/index.md` lines 9–59.
**Apply to:** `docs/index.md` 2×4 scheme grid + retargeted theme-gallery cards.

```markdown
<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   [![<preview alt>](<png path>)](<link target>)

    __[<card title>](<link target>)__

    ---

    <1–2 sentence card body>

-   [next card...]

</div>
```

Key invariants:
- `<div class="grid cards" markdown>` wrapper (the `markdown` attr enables md-in-html rendering).
- Each card = image link (clickable thumbnail) + bold-link title + `---` horizontal rule + single-paragraph body.
- Material theme auto-lays out 2-column grid on desktop, stacks on mobile.
- Placeholder tiles (no image, no link) just use bold heading + `---` + italic "Coming in Phase N" body.
