## Divergence Mechanism Choice

**Chose Option (b):** DIVERGENCE.md as human-readable methodology record + `tests/fixtures/divergences.yaml` as machine-readable test-input contract.

Rationale:
- DIVERGENCE.md carries structured tables, root-cause hypotheses, unlock procedure, and cross-references — readable by humans auditing the methodology.
- divergences.yaml is minimal YAML (year + category + reason + cross_ref per entry) consumed by the test at collection time via `_load_xfail_years()`.
- `test_divergences_yaml_sync` enforces: (a) no phantom years in divergences.yaml vs benchmarks.yaml, (b) no duplicates, (c) DIVERGENCE.md must exist while there are active entries.
- No try/except around the divergences.yaml read — file absence fails the test loudly, as required by the anti-patterns list.
